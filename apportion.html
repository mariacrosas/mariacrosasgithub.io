UNITED NATIONS. PEACEKEEPING OPERATIONS

How much does the peace cost?


Each Member State is obligated to pay their share for peacekeeping operations,

according to Article 17 of the Charter of the United Nations (link).

The Member States created a formula based on the relative economic wealth of each country.

The five permanent members of the Security Council are required to pay a higher amount

due to their responsibility to maintenance the international peace and security.
